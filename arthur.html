Tumwine Arthur Lutalo


born June 25th,1997) is a Ugandan, lawyer by professionand a business man. Arthur began his business career back in December, 2016.
This was his S.6 vacation when he started selling gadgets such as phones, laptops and their respective accessories.
By 2022, Lutalo had grown to be one of the best business men in Buziga working alongside men like Kissule Nathan who also
have been inspired by Arthur working hand in hand.
